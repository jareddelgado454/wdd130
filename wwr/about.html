White Water Rafting Co.

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and sustainable river adventures for people of all ages. Experienced guides, top-notch gear, and a passion for the river.

History

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting Co. started as a community project to bring responsible river experiences to local and international visitors. Over the years we expanded safety programs and grew our guided trip offerings.
