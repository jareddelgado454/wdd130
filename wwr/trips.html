Our Rafting Adventures

Explore Our Trips

Choose from a variety of thrilling white water rafting experiences, from peaceful scenic floats to high-adrenaline rapids. Whether you’re a beginner or an expert, we have the perfect trip for you!

Family Fun Float

Perfect for families and beginners, this gentle ride lets you enjoy the river’s beauty at a relaxed pace. Includes a riverside picnic and a friendly guide to ensure safety and fun for all ages.

Duration: 2 hours | Difficulty: Easy

Rapids Explorer

Our most popular trip! Enjoy class III-IV rapids with breathtaking canyon views. Designed for adventure seekers who want an exciting yet manageable challenge.

Duration: 4 hours | Difficulty: Moderate

Extreme Rapids Challenge

Not for the faint of heart! Conquer class IV-V rapids with our expert guides. Expect a powerful rush of adrenaline and unforgettable memories in the wildest sections of the river.

Duration: 6 hours | Difficulty: Hard
